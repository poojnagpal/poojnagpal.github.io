Times of India

During my second visit to India, a very unique situation occured. As I was teaching in one of the villages near Delhi, I saw a feature in the news on TV. It was covering a story about 40-50 girls who had dropped out of school due to harassment on the way. This shocked me. I immediately took an overnight train 7 hours to Bareilly and taught these girls.
